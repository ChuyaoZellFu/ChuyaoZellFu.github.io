Chuyao Fu

Undergraduate @ SUSTech Robotics and CV Lab | PKU HMI Lab

I am Chuyao Fu, a third-year undergraduate student in Electronic and Computer Engineering at the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech). My current research focuses on world models, vision-language-action models, and autonomous driving.

In SUSTech, I am supervised by Prof. Qinghu Meng and Prof. Hong Zhang. I am also a research intern at the PKU HMI Lab, supervised by Prof. Shanghang Zhang.

I am interested in the development of intelligent robotic systems. More concisely, I feel that my true interest lies in discovering the representations and structures that connect reliable perception with generalizable decision-making, especially in complex, dynamic environments.
